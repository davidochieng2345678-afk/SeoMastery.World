My Journey

It was 2 AM in Nairobi. I was staring at a Google Analytics screen showing zero visitors for the third month in a row. I had built a website. Written content. Done everything YouTube told me. And yet — nothing.

I almost gave up that night. But I didn't. Instead, I decided to figure out SEO from the ground up. No shortcuts. No expensive courses. Just testing, failing, learning, and testing again.

Months later, things started shifting. A client in Westlands found me on Google. Then a client in the UK. Then a Kenyan e-commerce brand whose traffic I tripled in 90 days. Then another. And another.

Today, I help businesses across Africa and the world dominate their search results — using the exact strategies in this book.
