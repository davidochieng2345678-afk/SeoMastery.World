About David Kiruo

SEO strategist, author, and founder of DK Digital — helping businesses rank higher, faster.

David Ochieng Kiruo

SEO Expert & Founder of DK Digital

David Kiruo is a Nairobi-based SEO expert, digital strategist, and founder of DK Digital. Born on February 11, 1988, he developed the proprietary 21-Day RAK System (Crawl Storm, Rapid Indexing, Authority Boost) after a decade of hands-on experience. He is the author of SEO Mastery: The Complete 90-Day Roadmap, a 300+ page digital guide designed to help entrepreneurs rank websites quickly and sustainably.

David Ochieng Kiruo is a Kenyan-born SEO specialist and the driving force behind DK Digital, a premier SEO agency headquartered in Nairobi. Born on February 11, 1988, David has spent over ten years decoding search engine algorithms, helping businesses—from local startups to international brands—achieve top Google rankings.

Frustrated by complex, theoretical SEO guides that failed to deliver practical results, David synthesized his real-world experience into a powerful, results-driven framework: the 21-Day RAK System. This unique methodology, which combines Crawl Storm, Rapid Indexing, and Authority Boost, is the backbone of his debut book, SEO Mastery: The Complete 90-Day Roadmap.

Published digitally via dkworldkenya.online, the book offers a no-fluff, step-by-step blueprint for turning any website into a traffic-generating machine. With a mission to bring Kenyan expertise to the global digital stage, David continues to consult, write, and speak on SEO strategy while running his agency at dkdigitalseo.online.
